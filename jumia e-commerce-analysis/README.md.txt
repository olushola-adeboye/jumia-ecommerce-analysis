## Project Overview
This project analyzes product data from **Jumia Nigeria**, one of the largest online marketplaces in Nigeria. The goal is to understand pricing patterns, discounts, and customer ratings across different product categories.

The project involves:
- Scraping product data from Jumia
- Cleaning and preprocessing the data
- Visualizing insights on prices, discounts, ratings, and categories
- Building a **Streamlit dashboard** for interactive exploration
